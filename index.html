Evidence for Evolution

Overview

Charles Darwin

1809-1882

An English naturalist and biologist who was the first to propose the idea of Evolution to the scientific community

Evolution

[ˌevəˈlo͞oSH(ə)n]

NOUN - The process by which different kinds of living organisms are thought to have developed and diversified from earlier forms during the history of the earth.

Natural Selection

Terminology

NOUN - The process whereby organisms better adapted to their environment tend to survive and produce more offspring. The theory of its action was first fully expounded by Charles Darwin and is now believed to be the main process that brings about evolution.
