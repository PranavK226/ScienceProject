Evolution

[ˌevəˈlo͞oSH(ə)n]

NOUN - The process by which different kinds of living organisms are thought to have developed and diversified from earlier forms during the history of the earth.

Evidences for Evolution

Terminology

Different methods and scientific observations that help scientists determine how species evolved over time.

Comparative Anatomy

Definition

Comparative Anatomy is a type of evidence for evolution that looks at the similar anatomy between different species, and correlates them. This is a mostly an observation.
